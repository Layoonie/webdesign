1. Open xD
  2. Create an artboard that is 500 by 500px.
  3. Select the Ellipse tool and draw a big circle filling up the first bottom 1/3 of the artboard.
  4. Draw another circle directly above your previously drawn circle, slightly smaller, filling up the middle 1/3 of the artboard.
  5. Draw your final circle directly above the previously drawn circle, slightly smaller, filling up the top 1/3 of the artboard.
  6. Select all three circles and change the fill to #C1E5FF with no border.
  7. Select the Ellipse tool and create a smaller circle over the big circle at the bottom of the artboard that is half the size.
  8. Create another circle in the middle circle that is half the size of that circle.
  9. Create your final circle in the small circle at the top of the page, half the size of that circle as well.
  10. Select all three of the new circles you just created and change the fill to #FFFFFF, with no borders.
  11. Export as png.
  12. Email image file to lalshahr@gmu.edu
